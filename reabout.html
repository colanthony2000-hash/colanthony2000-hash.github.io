The Beginning

Founded Inside the Classroom

Jeneconk didn't start as a tech company. It started in a classroom. In 2011, our founder saw a gap, schools were growing, but their administrative systems were stuck in the past.

We began by training teachers and setting up basic ICT labs. But we quickly realized that training wasn't enough. Schools needed systems. They needed a way to turn months of manual work into minutes of automated processing.
