School planning

How to prepare a scheme of work for the term.

A scheme of work turns the curriculum into a practical weekly teaching plan. It helps teachers pace topics, prepare resources, and avoid rushed coverage.

Planning method

Work from curriculum to calendar.

List the curriculum topics first, then map them to the number of teaching weeks available. Remove public holidays, examination weeks, revision periods, and school events before assigning topics.

For each week, define the topic, learning objectives, teaching resources, activities, and assessment method. A useful scheme should make lesson-note preparation easier and help school leaders monitor coverage.

Do not simply divide topics equally. Some topics need more time, demonstrations, practice, or revision. Build in breathing space for difficult areas.
