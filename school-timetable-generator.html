Timetable planning checklist

A good timetable balances learning, staff load, and real school constraints.

Teacher availabilityAvoid assigning one teacher to two classes at the same time.
Subject rhythmSpread demanding subjects across the week instead of crowding them into one day.
Breaks and assemblyLock non-teaching blocks before placing normal subject periods.
Review before publishingLet class teachers and administrators review the timetable before printing or sending to parents.
