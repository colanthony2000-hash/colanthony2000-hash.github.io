Frequently asked

Quick answers.

Is a scheme of work the same as a lesson note?

No. A scheme maps the term or period; a lesson note expands a specific lesson for classroom delivery.

Can Edu Suite adapt schemes to different formats?

Edu Suite supports multiple lesson and planning structures, with teacher review for school-specific requirements.
